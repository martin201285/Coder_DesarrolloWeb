About

The Artist

Martin Brown is a musician, producer and composer from Buenos Aires, Argentina. His main instrument is the guitar but he can play other instruments like bass and keyboards.

He worked in musical projects and bands like Zelion, Ambar, Artificial Intelligence, Sebastian Persini's solo album and Disrupted Mind.

As a solo artist, he composes, plays and programs all the tracks for his songs.

The musical genres in wich he stands out are rock and metal, but he can release songs from other styles.
